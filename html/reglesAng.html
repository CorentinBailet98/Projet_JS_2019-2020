Wrestling Game

Press Space to go back Translate to English press "T"

  • The goal of the game is simple, you have to get your opponent out of the red circle (3 times to win). Or to recover the most boosts.
    For this purpose, boosts will appear randomly on the carpet. You can accumulate up to 3 boosts at most.
    A match that lasts 1/2 minutes If at the end of the time both players are out the same number of times, the winner is the one who has to accumulate the most boosts.
    • The different functions of the boost:
      (number of boost(s) to be used)
    • With 1 boost you can make an acceleration on a short distance.
    • With 2 boosts you can make a switch, the switch allows you (during a collision with the opponent) to exchange your position with the opponent's while keeping the same direction of travel.
    • With 3 boosts it is possible to make a propulsion that sends the opponent far enough away. Both players will be "knocked out" after the attack which means that you have to press on a button to get move again.
